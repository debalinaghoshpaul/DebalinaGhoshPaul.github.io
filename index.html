Dr. Debalina Ghosh Paul

I completed my Doctor of Philosophy (PhD), focusing on Testing and Evaluation of Large Language Models (LLMs) in Code Generation in Feb 2026, from the Institute of AI, Data Analysis and Systems (AIDAS), Oxford Brookes University, England, United Kingdom under the supervision of Prof. Hong Zhu and Dr. Ian Bayley.

I am a researcher dedicated to bridging the gap between AI generation and industry-standard software reliability. My work addresses the "black box" nature of LLMs by pioneering datamorphic, scenario-based testing frameworks that move beyond simple accuracy metrics toward scenario-aware, multi-attribute, automated verification.

My unique background, transitioning from a foundation in Cybersecurity and Cryptography to Generative AI reliability, allows me to approach AI-generated code with a "security-first" mindset. I have developed scalable benchmarks, ScenEval, and test systems that directly address technical debt, robustness, reliability and other quality dimensions in AI-assisted programming. As the UK moves toward its 2026 AI Regulatory Framework, my research provides the essential verification methodologies required for high-stakes sectors like Finance and Healthcare to deploy AI with confidence.
